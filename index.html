How to Play

1. Goal: Guess the other´s Secret Character by Asking them Questions about the Character!

2. Eliminate: Left-click on a card to flip it down if the character doesn't match your opponent's description.

3. Secret Card: Right-click on any card to set your own secret character at the start of the round.

4. Win: The Player who guessed the other´s Secret Card Wins! (if you guess and you are wrong its an instant loose!)
